I The Artist
Portrait

The hand behind the ink.

Ego Inkk has been tattooing in Chicago since 2016. Over a thousand pieces and counting.

He apprenticed at 7 Shades of Gray in Oak Park and built his name on black and gray: work that reads clean on every skin tone, with real care for tattooing melanated skin. Cover-ups are where he shows out, redirecting the eye and turning a piece you regret into one you want to wear proud.

Every session is one on one in a private, climate-controlled studio. The work is personal, and it is built to last.

II The Style

Black and gray is home base.

Versatile across realism and color when a piece calls for it, but black and gray is the strong suit and cover-ups are where the real magic happens.

i

Black & Gray

The strong suit. Done right, it reads on everybody, from the lightest skin to the darkest. It all comes down to understanding how the skin works.

ii

Cover-Ups

Where he shows out. A cover-up redirects the eye and turns a piece you regret into one you want to show off.

iii

Realism & Color

Versatile with realism and color, using opaques that hold up and show up on melanated skin.

iv

Trash Polka

Bold red and black graphic realism. Abstract slashes and splatter collide with sharp portrait work for something loud and unmistakable.
